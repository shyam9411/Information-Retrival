Hurricane is the first single by the singer-songwriter Natalie Grant from her album Hurricane which was released on October 15, 2013.[2]

Background[edit]

The song was written by Matt Bronleewe, Natalie Grant, Cindy Morgan.

Music video[edit]

The lyric video for the song premiered on April 29, 2013 on YouTube.
