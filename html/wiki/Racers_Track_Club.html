Racers Track Club

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Racers Track Club is a sprint training group based in Kingston, Jamaica established by Glen Mills with the stated aim of producing a greater number of world champions than any other track club.[1] The club is known for training some of the world's best sprinters, world champions currently training with the group include Usain Bolt and Yohan Blake.[2]
