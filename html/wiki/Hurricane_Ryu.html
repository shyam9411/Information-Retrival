Hurricane Ryu

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

Hariken Ryu (破李拳竜, Hariken Ryu, born January 22, 1958), nicknamed Hurricane Ryu, is a Japanese illustrator, comic book author, writer, director, martial artist and an actor.[1] His real name is Hidemi Miyata, born in Tochigi Prefecture.
