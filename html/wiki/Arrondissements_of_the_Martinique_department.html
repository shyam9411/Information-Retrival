Arrondissements of the Martinique department

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The 4 arrondissements of the Martinique department are:

arrondissements of the Martinique
  1. Arrondissement of Fort-de-France, (prefecture of the Martinique department: Fort-de-France) with 4 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 161,021 in 2013.
  2. Arrondissement of La Trinité, (subprefecture: La Trinité) with 10 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 81,475 in 2013.
  3. Arrondissement of Le Marin, (subprefecture: Le Marin) with 12 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 119,653 in 2013.
  4. Arrondissement of Saint-Pierre, (subprefecture: Saint-Pierre) with 8 communes. The population of the arrondissement was 23,402 in 2013.
