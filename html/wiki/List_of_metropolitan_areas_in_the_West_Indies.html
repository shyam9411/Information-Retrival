List of metropolitan areas in the West Indies

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

This is a list of the largest metropolitan areas in the West Indies, based on official population estimates or projections as of mid-2015. Havana has no officially sanctioned definition of its metropolitan area; the population within its city limits (La Habana Province) is given instead.

Metropolitan area Population Year Country
1 Santo Domingo 3,658,648[1] 2015  Dominican Republic
2 Port-au-Prince 2,618,894[2] 2015  Haiti
3 San Juan 2,196,538[3] 2015  Puerto Rico
4 Havana 2,117,625[4] 2015  Cuba
5 Kingston 1,190,763[a][6] 2015  Jamaica
6 Santiago de los Caballeros 891,674[b][8] 2015  Dominican Republic

Notes[edit]

  1. ^ The National Works Agency of Jamaica defines the Kingston Metropolitan Region as comprising the parishes of Kingston, Saint Catherine and Saint Andrew.[5]
  2. ^ In 2015, Reynaldo Peguero, the director of the Santiago Development Council (Consejo de Desarrollo de Santiago, CDES), defined the metropolitan area of Santiago as comprising the municipalities of Santiago proper, Baitoa, Licey al Medio, Puñal, Tamboril and Villa González.[7]
