"Hurricane" was the lead single released from The Click's second album, Game Related.

Produced by Studio Ton, the song became The Click's most successful single, peaking at number 63 on the Billboard Hot 100 and number four on the Billboard Hot Rap Singles. The song's title comes from the alcoholic drink of the same name. The background vocals feature a little know artist named Sapphire from Cleveland, Ohio. She now manages the rapper Critikal.
