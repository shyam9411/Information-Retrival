Washington Misick (born 1950) is a politician from the Turks and Caicos Islands. He served as the island territory's Chief Minister[1] from April 1991 to 31 January 1995.

He was re-elected to the Turks and Caicos Islands House of Assembly as an at-large member in the 2012 elections.
